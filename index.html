Tyre Compounds

Pit Stops
Each stop costs ~23s. Fewer stops = less time lost — but worn tyres = slow laps. That's the core trade-off.

A Safety Car, VSC, or Rain may hit mid-race. A SC can hand you a free pit stop — or give Remin one. Timing is everything.
